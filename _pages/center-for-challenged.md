This inclusive playground installation in Tulsa, Oklahoma was completed by Happy Playgrounds for the **Center for Individuals with Physical Challenges**, creating an accessible and engaging outdoor space for individuals of all abilities.

The project was designed with a strong focus on **inclusive and adaptive play**, ensuring that children and individuals with physical challenges can safely participate, interact, and enjoy the benefits of outdoor recreation.

Working closely with the client, Happy Playgrounds helped deliver a custom solution that promotes independence, social connection, and physical activity in a supportive and welcoming environment.

This project reflects our commitment to designing spaces that bring communities together and provide meaningful play experiences for everyone.

Happy Playgrounds provides commercial playground equipment, shade structures, and inclusive design services across Oklahoma and Arkansas for schools, municipalities, churches, and community organizations.
