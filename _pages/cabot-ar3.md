This playground installation in Cabot, Arkansas was completed by Happy Playgrounds to create a safe, engaging, and durable outdoor play environment for students.

The project was designed to support active play, social interaction, and long-term usability, ensuring children have access to a high-quality playground space that promotes both physical activity and imaginative play.

Happy Playgrounds worked closely with the client to deliver a custom playground solution tailored to the needs of the site while maintaining safety, accessibility, and long-term performance.

Happy Playgrounds provides commercial playground equipment, shade structures, design, and installation services across Oklahoma and Arkansas for schools, municipalities, churches, and community organizations.
