This playground installation in Tahlequah, Oklahoma—we affectionately call the **Uwoduhi Durbin Feeling Playground** (meaning “beautiful” in Cherokee)—was designed and completed by Happy Playgrounds to create a safe, engaging, and inclusive outdoor environment for the local community.

The project includes **USA Shade structures**, providing protection from the elements while enhancing comfort and usability for children and families.

Happy Playgrounds provides commercial playground equipment, shade structures, design, and installation services across Oklahoma and Arkansas for schools, municipalities, churches, and general contractors.
