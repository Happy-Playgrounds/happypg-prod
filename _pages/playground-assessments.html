Playground Assessments

"Taking a proactive approach to reviewing your playground helps you stay ahead of maintenance needs and plan for the future." Kelli Collins

Every playground experiences wear over time. Our team provides playground assessments to give you a clear picture of your site’s current condition and help identify areas that may need attention.

We review equipment, surfacing, and overall layout to better understand how your space is performing and where improvements or updates could enhance usability and longevity. Our approach is practical and focused on helping you make informed decisions about maintenance, repairs, or future enhancements.

Following each assessment, we provide a clear and easy-to-understand summary of our observations along with recommended next steps. This allows you to prioritize improvements, extend the life of your playground, and continue providing a great experience for your community.
